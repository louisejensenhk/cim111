This is where I grew up.


Yo, I LOVE Hong Kong, I swear when you come to Hong Kong you would want to live there. The view is amazing. Hong Kong is a city that I miss the most, and that I will always remember.

The view is amazing at the Peak. One place you need to go to when you are visiting Hong Kong, is The Peak. The Peak also offers a very good hike that you can go on. I like it a lot and it's not that long. Don't worry.

If you come to Hong Kong and you are looking for a beach to go swimming, you should visit The Repulse Bay Beach. The Repulse Bay Beach is a cool beach. Lot's of people have fun there. You can ask anyone. If you are in Hong Kong when it is Lantern Festival, the Repulse Bay beach is a great place to experience the Chinese lunar moon festival. It's a great place to go with your friends and Family. But it's dark so tell your children not to go swimming or they'll get lost!
Hope you Enjoy Hong Kong!!!!
