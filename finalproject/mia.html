Miami's Adventures

Beautiful unknown adventures I've been on, Dec 3, 2018


1. Key Largo Kayaking:


Key Largo is a census-designated place in Monroe County, Florida, United States, located on the island of Key Largo in the upper Florida Keys. The population was 10,433 at the 2010 census. The name comes from the Spanish Cayo Largo, or "long key". It's only 40 minutes away from Miami and you can drive along U.S. 1 highway.



2. Okeechobee Music & Arts Festival:


The Okeechobee Music and Arts Festival was an annual four-day music and arts festival produced by Soundslinger, LLC. The festival was held at the 600-acre property, Sunshine Grove in Okeechobee, Florida.
